LeoStats

LeoStats is a compact & simple dashboard to better inform FIRST Robotics teams about their schedules, placements, alliances, & more during an event.

It's built using JavaScript & Python, with a webserver running on Flask. It gathers its data from the FIRST Events API and sorts/displays that data in an interface curated for convinience.

https://github.com/FRC-4459/LeoStats

RemindBot ü§ñ

RemindBot is a finished project which aims to connect LSHS STEM students further with their teachers through connection of the Remind and Discord platforms.

The goal of this bot is to allow teachers to opt-in to automatically sending their Remind messages to the official LSHS STEM Discord server. Teachers simply have to add the bot to their class, and the bot forwards their messages to its selected server.

https://github.com/kingstonv1/remindBot
